The Art of Swiss Ice Cream

Every Mövenpick flavour is natural, with no artificial additives or colours - a subtle and delicate work of culinary inspiration that is sure to delight the most discerning gourmets.

An ice cream born of a passion for gastronomy, blessed with the best that nature can provide and fashioned with Swiss perfection.

About Movenpick Ice Cream

Since the earliest days of Mövenpick Ice Cream, gastronomy has always been our guiding passion. You can see that passion in the quality of the ingredients used, always searching the world for the best.

Our products use only the finest ingredients, always selected with the greatest care and always processed in a natural manner with no artificial colours or flavourings. The reward is a subtlety of flavours and textures that no artificial colours can match.

In New Zealand, Mövenpick is distributed by Auckland based Emerald Foods Ltd.

For any Foodservice related enquiries, please contact Emerald Foods Ltd. For all other enquiries, please contact us.
